However, they had to ride past the grave, and there, on the hazel tree, sat the two pigeons, crying out: Rook di goo, rook di goo! There's blood in the shoe. He turned his horse around and took the false bride home again. The other sister tried on the shoe but her heel was too large. Then her mother gave her a knife, and said, "Cut a piece off your heel. When you are queen you will no longer have to go on foot." The girl cut a piece off her heel and went out to the prince. He took her on his horse as his bride and rode away with her.
